In the quiet of the fields,
under a blanket of stars,
I watched the year grow,
much like the grass beneath our feet.
They say ladybugs are a sign of luck,
a small spark in a wide world.
To me, that spark has always been you.
As the clock resets and the sky ignites,
I have just one wish for us...
The sky is awake now,
painted with light and noise.
But down here in the grass,
the world is still ours.
Let this new year bring you
wings to fly and ground to stand on.
Let every day be a discovery.
HAPPY NEW YEAR!
Here's to 2026.
